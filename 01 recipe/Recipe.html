How to make Pupusas

    • Ingredients

      1 block of mozzarella cheese
      small packet of pork
      1.5 small tomato
      1/2 of an onion
      1/2 green pepper
      Pinch of salt
      1 cup of water
      2 cups of Maseca brand flour
      Prep Time: 30 mins Cook Time: 2.5 hours
    1. Dice up vegetables into medium sizes placing the green pepper in a seperate bowl
    2. In a medium size bowl grate cheese and green pepper
    3. Kneed it together with a bit of salt making sure it gets mixed well
    4. Cut up the packet of pork in small chunksand wash it thoroughly
    5. Place in medium size pot place pork over a low medium fire and cover
    6. Check occasionally and stir it to keep pork from sticking
    7. Once the pork is tender take cover off,add salt and stir
    8. Let pork sit on fire until golden brown on a medium fire
    9. Place pork,tomato, and onion in meat grinder
    10. Once all of it is ground kneed together
    11. In a medium bowl add the Maseca and water kneeding it until its a solid doughy consistency
    12. Grab about a handful of dough and make it into a ball adding water to your hand so it wont stick
    13. In the center of the ball choose between filling it with;cheese, meat, or both
    14. Close the ball removing excess dough and role untill filling is completly covered
    15. Begin flattening out the ball to a tortilla shape, rotating it so filling is evenly spread through out
    16. Place the comal over a medium fire until hot *to check splash water on it if it sizzle the comal is hot enough for the pupusas
    17. Place the pupusas on it giving space between one another
    18. Let it sit on heat until it goldens then flip
    19. Once the other side is golden, take it off the heat
    20. Enjoy!
    *Ingredients amounts may vary based
